# Monetization Guide

## Revenue Models

### 1. CPM (Cost Per Mille)
- Earn per 1000 ad impressions
- Typical rate: $0.50 - $5.00 per 1000 impressions
- Best for: High-traffic links

### 2. CPC (Cost Per Click)
- Earn per click on ads
- Typical rate: $0.01 - $0.50 per click
- Best for: Targeted traffic

### 3. Revenue Share
- Platform takes 30%, creators get 70%
- Transparent and fair model
- Encourages quality content

## Google AdSense Setup

1. **Apply for AdSense**
   - Go to google.com/adsense
   - Apply with your domain
   - Wait for approval (1-3 weeks)

2. **Get Client ID**
   - In AdSense dashboard, go to Settings
   - Copy your Client ID
   - Add to environment variables

3. **Create Ad Units**
   - Create 300x250 (Medium Rectangle) ad unit
   - Copy the ad slot ID
   - Update in `app/s/[shortCode]/page.tsx`

4. **Optimize Placement**
   - Place ads above the fold
   - Use responsive ad sizes
   - Test different placements

## Optimization Tips

### Increase CTR (Click-Through Rate)
- Make ads relevant to content
- Use compelling ad copy
- Test different ad formats
- Optimize landing pages

### Increase CPM
- Target high-value countries (US, UK, CA, AU)
- Focus on quality traffic
- Use contextual targeting
- Build audience trust

### Reduce Bounce Rate
- Fast page load times
- Clear call-to-action
- Mobile-friendly design
- Relevant destination URLs

## Analytics to Track

- **Impressions**: Total ad views
- **Clicks**: Total ad clicks
- **CTR**: Click-through rate (clicks/impressions)
- **CPM**: Cost per 1000 impressions
- **Earnings**: Total revenue
